Paper Submission

All submissions will be handled electronically via the workshop's CMT paper submission website. Papers are limited to eight pages, including figures and tables, in the CVPR style. Additional pages containing only cited references are allowed. A complete paper should be submitted using the CVPR templates, which are blind-submission review-formatted templates.
